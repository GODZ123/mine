Marian College Kuttikanam

Marian College Kuttikkanam (Marian College) was established in the year 1995. The campus is spread over 27 acres of land. The College is approved and granted by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the University Grants Commission (UGC). The College offers undergraduate (BCA/BBA/BSW/B.Com/BA/B.Sc) and postgraduate (MCA/M.Com/MSW/MA CMS/MMH/M.Phil/PGDGCM) courses in different areas. Marian College is affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University Kottayam.
